Key Solana Advantages for Your Use Case:
1. Parallel Processing:

Agents can mint/redeem simultaneously

No gas wars or network congestion

2. Low Fees:

Mint/Redeem operations cost ~$0.0001

Sustainable for micro-transactions

3. Native Token-2022 Features:

Transfer hooks for automatic fee collection

On-chain metadata for compliance

Custom extensions for advanced logic

4. PDA Security:

Treasury controlled by program, not EOAs

No private key management for protocol funds
